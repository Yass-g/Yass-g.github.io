About me

I am a 2nd year PhD student at DIKU under the supervision of Jakob Nordström as part of the MIAO group. I am mainly working on proof complexity and broadly interested in computational complexity. Prior to joining the University of Copenhagen, I completed an MSc. in EECS at Cornell University in 2023 (as my 4A at Polytechnique) and graduated from Ecole Polytechnique in 2022 Majoring in Computer science. Further back, I completed two years of preparatory program (CPGE) at Lycée d'Excellence Mohammed VI (previously Lydex).
